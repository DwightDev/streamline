How to use this editor

Edit the Case Study Master page directly in the browser, save versions, then export clean HTML.

  1. Click any outlined text on the page and type your changes.
  2. Select text, then use Bold, Italic, Bullet list, or the color palette.
  3. Use ☰ Drafts to save a version. Drafts can be loaded, renamed, or deleted.
  4. Use Edit link while your cursor is inside a button/link to change its URL.
  5. Use Preview to temporarily hide the editor toolbar and outlines. Press Esc to return.
  6. Use Download clean HTML when you are ready to export the page without editor controls.
Drafts are saved in this browser using localStorage. They will work tomorrow on the same computer/browser, but they will not automatically sync to another device.

Their story

A modern interface that made sense from day one.

The first thing that caught the Capital Bread team's eye was the interface. In a market where bakery software often feels dated or overly complex, Streamline stood out immediately.

"The software is very intuitive. I found my way around the system very quickly," says Francois Bonnefoy, Managing Director and Co-Founder of Capital Bread Ltd.

"We were seduced by your platform — the layout, everything was logical to us." That first impression led to a decision that has supported Capital Bread's continued growth.

Every bakery is different: finding a solution that fits within — or adapts to — their operation was the main priority. Streamline delivered on both fronts. Capital Bread were able to shape the system around their workflows rather than the other way around.

Better reporting. Clearer vision.

One of the early wins for Capital Bread was reporting. Before Streamline, getting a clear view of operations required piecing together data from multiple places. The reporting in Streamline changed that.

"We all expect everything to run very smoothly and never be a problem. For peace of mind, I like that it's on the cloud: if data is corrupted or there are any problems, there's always a backup."

"The reporting is good, the vision."

"I love the interface. It's so in date with its time." — Francois Bonnefoy, Capital Bread

Growth supported by Mountain Stream.

For a growing wholesale bakery, having a software partner that stays close matters. Capital Bread have experienced exactly that.

"Mountain Stream supports us with our growth" — a reflection of a relationship that goes beyond software support into genuine partnership.

With 24/7 cover, cloud-based data security, and a team that understands bakery operations, Capital Bread have a system they can rely on as they scale.
