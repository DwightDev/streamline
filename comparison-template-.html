The key difference

Connected ERP workflows from order to invoice.

Cybake is established bakery management software with useful wholesale, retail, production, invoicing, delivery, and integration features. Streamline stands out when a bakery needs one connected operational system across customer ordering, production, inventory, traceability, delivery, finance, CRM, reporting, and customer service.

🛒

Wholesale ordering depth

Streamline supports standing orders, cart orders, saved carts, cloned orders, late orders, customer-specific catalogues, pricing, SKUs, lead times, order deadlines, address permissions, and mobile ordering.

🏭

Production tied to inventory

Orders can drive recipe totals, recipe cards, ingredient demand, batch splitting, inventory depletion, finished goods, allergens, nutrition facts, labels, and lot records.

🚚

Delivery and finance stay connected

Route planning, packing slips, proof of delivery, reusable delivery assets, unprocessed invoices, credit notes, statements, payment collection, and accounting workflows all connect back to the same operational data.

FAQ

Common questions about Streamline vs Cybake.

What is the best Cybake alternative for wholesale bakeries?
Streamline is a strong Cybake alternative for wholesale bakeries that need connected ordering, standing orders, production planning, inventory, traceability, route planning, proof of delivery, invoicing, CRM, accounting workflows, and reporting in one bakery ERP system.
Is Streamline bakery ERP software?
Yes. Streamline is bakery ERP software for wholesale bakeries, food manufacturers, and distributors. It connects order management, production, recipes, inventory, purchasing, traceability, labelling, allergens, nutrition facts, delivery, invoicing, accounts receivable, CRM, reporting, and customer service workflows.
Does Streamline handle traceability?
Yes. Streamline tracks lots from supplier goods-in through recipes, finished goods, packing slips, customer deliveries, and recall workflows. This helps bakeries and food manufacturers connect ingredient traceability with production, delivery, and customer records.
Which platform is better for retail bakery shops?
Cybake may be a good fit for retail-focused bakeries because it includes shop ordering, EPOS-connected workflows, retail waste control, stock reconciliation, and in-store production planning. Streamline is stronger when retail is part of a larger operation that also needs connected wholesale, production, inventory, delivery, finance, CRM, and reporting workflows.
