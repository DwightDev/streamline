FAQ

Bakery ERP feature questions, answered.

What is Streamline?

Streamline is bakery ERP software that helps wholesale bakeries manage online ordering, production planning, recipe management, inventory, lot traceability, distribution, invoicing, finance, reporting, and integrations.

Does Streamline include production planning software?

Yes. Streamline uses order quantities, current stock, par levels, forecasts, maximum stock limits, recipes, labels, and inventory data to help bakeries plan production.

Does Streamline include inventory management?

Yes. Streamline supports goods-in, purchase invoice scanning, lot assignment, barcode labels, inventory moves, stock counts, stock adjustments, recipe production, ingredient depletion, and replenishment.

Does Streamline support traceability?

Yes. Streamline tracks lots from vendor purchase information through goods-in, production, recipe lots, packing slips, dispatch, and recall communication.
