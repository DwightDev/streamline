FAQ

What does Goods Receipt do in the Streamline Inventory App?

It helps users scan invoices, confirm delivered quantities, assign lot numbers, record use-by dates and delivery temperature, and update stock from a mobile receiving workflow.

Can it assign lot numbers when goods are received?

Yes. The Inventory App context shows that an internal lot number can be generated on receipt, with vendor lot references linked for traceability.

Does Goods Receipt update stock levels?

Yes. Once goods are received and confirmed, stock levels are updated so inventory reflects the received goods.

Is this separate from Stock Counting and Recipe Production?

Yes. Goods Receipt handles goods-in receiving. Stock Counting and Recipe Production are separate key Inventory App features.
