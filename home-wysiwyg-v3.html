How to use this editor

Simple workflow for editing, saving drafts, and exporting clean HTML.

  1. Edit text: click any outlined copy on the page and type your changes.
  2. Format text: select text, then use Bold, Italic, Bullet list, or a color swatch.
  3. Edit links: click inside a button or link, then choose Edit link.
  4. Save progress: open ☰ Drafts and click Save current version. You can rename, load, or delete saved versions from the same menu.
  5. Preview: click Preview to hide the editor toolbar. Press Esc to return.
  6. Export final: click Download clean HTML when you are ready to upload or use the edited page.

What is Streamline ERP?

Streamline ERP is flexible business management software for order-driven companies. It helps teams manage online ordering, customer accounts, CRM, catalogues, production planning, recipes or bills of materials, inventory, lot tracking, traceability, delivery, invoicing, payment collection, accounting exports, reporting, automation, and integrations.

One database. Every team.

Orders, production, inventory, finance, and reporting share the same operational data — eliminating the re-entry and handoffs that break disconnected tools.

FAQ

Questions buyers ask before choosing ERP software.

What is Streamline ERP?

Streamline ERP is flexible business software from Mountain Stream for order-driven operations. It connects customer ordering, production planning, recipes or bills of materials, inventory, lot tracking, traceability, distribution, invoicing, finance, reporting, and integrations.

Is Streamline only for bakeries?

No. Bakery remains a proven niche and an important source of operational expertise, but the platform supports broader workflows used by food producers, distributors, commercial kitchens, prepared-food businesses, and specialty manufacturers.

Which companies are the best fit?

Streamline is a strong fit for businesses with recurring customers, customer-specific pricing, production planning, stock movement, traceability requirements, route delivery, invoices, payment collection, and operational reporting.

Does Streamline support inventory and traceability?

Yes. Streamline supports receiving goods, assigning lot numbers, printing labels, moving stock, making products, depleting ingredients, tracing lots, and connecting traceability records to packing slips and recall communication.

Can Streamline integrate with accounting and delivery tools?

Yes. Streamline integrates with QuickBooks, Xero, Stripe, WholeSail, Melio, ACH, OptimoRoute, Detrack, and Shopify.

How is Streamline different from generic ERP software?

Streamline is purpose-built for order-driven production and distribution. Standing orders, recipe management, lot traceability, route delivery, and wholesale invoicing are standard features — not expensive configuration projects that take months to implement.
