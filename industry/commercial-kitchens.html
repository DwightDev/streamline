What it does

Run commercial kitchen operations from one connected ERP workflow.

Commercial kitchens do not just need recipe binders, production spreadsheets, stock sheets, and separate invoice tools. They need customer demand, recipes, ingredients, production, inventory, packing, delivery, finance, and reporting to stay connected every day.

Streamline brings those moving parts into one operational workflow for commissaries, central kitchens, prepared food teams, wholesale food producers, and route-based distributors, so the office, kitchen, warehouse, drivers, and finance team work from the same demand and inventory data.

What is commercial kitchen ERP software? Commercial kitchen ERP software connects the systems a food production team uses to run daily operations: customer ordering, standing orders, production planning, recipes, ingredient inventory, lot tracking, packing, delivery, invoicing, payment collection, accounting exports, and reporting.

Order

Receive recurring, cart, mobile, CSV, SFTP, EDI, wholesale, or multi-location orders.

Plan

Convert demand into prep quantities, Recipe Totals, production dates, shopping lists, and ingredient needs.

Prep

Use recipe cards, sub-recipes, stages, machines, labels, allergens, and costs to guide daily kitchen work.

Track

Control ingredients, supplier lots, internal lots, use-by dates, FEFO, and finished goods.

Deliver

Pick, pack, route, capture POD, track delivery assets, and keep customer delivery records.

Invoice

Create invoices from deliveries, collect payments, export accounts, and report on margins.

Finance & Reporting

Connect kitchen output to invoices, payments, and accounting exports.

Streamline helps commercial kitchens reduce re-entry between delivery, finance, and accounting. Completed deliveries and customer pricing can flow into draft invoices, published invoices, customer invoice libraries, payment collection, and accounting exports.

  • Automatic draft invoices from deliveries and pricing
  • Daily, weekly, or monthly invoice frequencies
  • Customer invoice library, statements, and credit notes
  • Accounts receivable and overdue balance tracking
  • Stripe payment collection and invoice payment links
  • QuickBooks and Xero-oriented accounting export workflows
  • Gross profit reporting from recipe and item costs
  • Custom reporting for orders, production, inventory, delivery, and finance

Why it helps

When customer demand, production, delivery, and pricing stay connected, finance teams spend less time rebuilding invoices and more time reviewing exceptions, collecting payments, and closing the loop with accounting.

FAQ

Commercial kitchen ERP software questions, answered.

What is commercial kitchen ERP software?

Commercial kitchen ERP software connects the workflows a central kitchen, commissary, or prepared food team uses to manage customer orders, production planning, recipes, ingredients, inventory, lot traceability, delivery, invoicing, payments, accounting exports, and reporting.

How does Streamline help commercial kitchens?

Streamline helps commercial kitchens manage recurring customer orders, cart orders, price lists, recipes, production planning, ingredient demand, inventory tracking, lot numbers, packing, delivery documents, invoices, payments, and reporting from one connected system.

Can Streamline connect customer orders to kitchen production?

Yes. Customer order demand can flow into production planning, Recipe Totals, Recipe Cards, ingredient demand, inventory updates, production dates, and finished goods workflows, helping teams plan what needs to be made based on real demand and available stock.

Does Streamline support recipe costing and labels?

Yes. Streamline supports recipe costs, item costs, ingredient usage, nutrition facts, allergen roll-ups, labels, recipe cards, yield logic, and finished goods records for production teams.

Can Streamline help with ingredient inventory and traceability?

Yes. Streamline supports goods-in, suppliers, storage locations, stock counts, stock adjustments, ingredients, lot details, use-by dates, FEFO stock selection, production usage, finished goods, delivery records, and recall-ready traceability.

Can commercial kitchens use Streamline for delivery workflows?

Yes. Streamline includes distribution workflows such as Route Manager, Pick & Pack, delivery notes, packing slips, delivery charges, driver reports, route optimization integrations, and delivery asset tracking.

Is Streamline only for bakeries?

No. Bakery is a proven Streamline strength, but the platform is built as flexible ERP software for order-driven production and distribution businesses. That means it can also support commercial kitchens, central kitchens, commissaries, prepared food teams, food producers, and route-based distributors with similar workflow needs.
