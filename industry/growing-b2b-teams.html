What it does

Run repeat B2B orders without the admin pile-up.

Growing wholesale teams do not just need somewhere to enter orders. They need account rules, recurring demand, special pricing, delivery addresses, stock availability, invoices, and payments to stay in sync.

FAQ

B2B ERP software questions, answered.

Who is Streamline's B2B ERP software for?

Streamline is for B2B teams such as wholesale bakeries, food producers, commercial kitchens, commissaries, prepared food teams, and route-based distributors that manage repeat customers, special pricing, inventory, delivery, invoicing, payments, and reporting.

How does Streamline help with repeat B2B orders?

Streamline supports standing orders, cart orders, saved carts, cloned orders, mobile ordering, late order handling, CSV uploads, SFTP, and EDI order exchange.

Can Streamline handle customer-specific pricing and catalogues?

Yes. Streamline supports customer-specific price lists, item visibility, catalogues, customer SKUs, discount rules, and account-level order settings.

Can customers have multiple users, locations, and permissions?

Yes. Streamline supports customer contacts, invited users, module permissions, saved permission sets, address-level access, multiple delivery addresses, and customer-specific invoice options.

Does Streamline connect delivery and invoicing?

Yes. Delivery records, customer pricing, delivery notes, packing slips, proof of delivery, draft invoices, statements, payment records, and accounting exports can stay connected.

Is this only for bakery businesses?

No. Streamline has deep bakery and food-operation experience, but this page is for growing B2B teams that need ordering, pricing, inventory, delivery, finance, and reporting connected in one workflow.
