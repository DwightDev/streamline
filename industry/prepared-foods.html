Inventory & Traceability

Control perishables, lots, and use-by dates before they hit the line.

Prepared foods rely on fresh ingredients moving quickly. Streamline helps teams receive stock, assign lots, track use-by dates, count inventory, deplete ingredients through recipes, and trace finished food back to what was used.

  • Goods-in, supplier receiving, and ingredient records
  • Storage locations, stock counts, and adjustments
  • Supplier lots, internal lots, use-by dates, and FEFO support
  • Barcode labels for inventory and production workflows
  • Ingredient depletion when recipes are completed
  • Finished goods traceability for recall-ready records

FAQ

Prepared foods ERP software questions, answered.

What is prepared foods ERP software?

Prepared foods ERP software connects recurring meal orders, recipe prep, ingredient inventory, lot traceability, use-by labels, packing, route delivery, invoicing, and reporting for meal prep, commissary, central kitchen, and ready-to-eat operations.

How does Streamline help prepared food operations?

Streamline helps teams manage customer orders, recipes, prep planning, ingredient demand, lot tracking, labels, packing, delivery documents, invoices, payments, and reporting in one connected ERP workflow.

Can Streamline connect meal orders to prep planning?

Yes. Order demand can flow into production planning, Recipe Totals, Recipe Cards, ingredient demand, inventory updates, production dates, finished goods, packing, and delivery records.

Does Streamline support labels, allergens, and nutrition?

Yes. Streamline supports recipe costs, ingredient usage, nutrition facts, allergen roll-ups, labels, recipe cards, yield logic, and finished goods records.

Can Streamline help with ingredients, lots, and use-by dates?

Yes. Streamline supports receiving, suppliers, storage locations, stock counts, adjustments, lots, use-by dates, FEFO support, production usage, finished goods, delivery records, and traceability.

Can prepared food teams use Streamline for packing and delivery?

Yes. Streamline includes Pick & Pack, Route Manager, delivery notes, packing slips, delivery charges, driver reports, route optimization integrations, proof of delivery, and delivery asset tracking.

Is Streamline only for bakeries?

No. Streamline has strong bakery experience, but it also supports order-driven food production and distribution teams such as prepared food operations, meal prep producers, commissaries, central kitchens, and route-based food suppliers.
