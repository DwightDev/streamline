FAQ

Food route supplier ERP questions, answered.

What is food route supplier ERP software?

Food route supplier ERP software connects standing orders, customer pricing, inventory, Pick & Pack, route delivery, proof of delivery, delivery asset tracking, invoices, payments, accounting exports, and reporting for suppliers delivering on repeat routes.

How does Streamline help food route suppliers?

Streamline helps food route suppliers manage recurring orders, customer-specific pricing, route-ready picking, delivery notes, proof of delivery, delivery asset tracking, credits, invoices, payment collection, and reporting.

Does Streamline support direct store delivery workflows?

Yes. Streamline supports DSD-style workflows through customer ordering, Pick & Pack, Route Manager, delivery documents, driver reports, proof of delivery, reusable asset tracking, and delivery-to-invoice workflows.

Can Streamline connect route delivery to invoicing?

Yes. Delivered orders can flow into draft invoices using customer pricing, delivery charges, invoice frequency, and billing settings. Streamline also supports AR, statements, payment collection, accounting exports, and reporting.

Can food route suppliers track delivery assets?

Yes. Streamline supports delivery asset tracking for items such as trays, crates, or totes, helping teams see asset movement between the warehouse, drivers, customers, and delivery records.

Is Streamline only for bakeries?

No. Bakery is a proven Streamline strength, but the platform is built for order-driven production and distribution businesses, including food and beverage suppliers, route-based distributors, prepared food teams, and wholesale distributors.
