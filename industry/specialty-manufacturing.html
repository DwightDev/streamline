What it does

Keep custom production moving without spreadsheet handoffs.

Specialty manufacturers rarely run simple, one-size-fits-all production. Orders change by customer, variant, pack size, material, lot, assembly step, delivery date, and invoice rule. Streamline helps keep those moving parts connected without forcing teams to rebuild the plan in spreadsheets.

Streamline ERP connects the daily workflow: customer demand becomes production plans, product structures drive material needs, stock and lots flow into fulfillment, deliveries become invoices, and managers get clearer reporting on output, stock, margins, and customer activity.

What is specialty manufacturing ERP software? It connects customer ordering, product structures, production planning, material stock, lot tracking, picking, delivery, invoicing, payments, accounting exports, and reporting so specialty manufacturers can control work from demand to delivered product.

FAQ

Specialty manufacturing ERP questions, answered.

What is specialty manufacturing ERP software?

It connects customer orders, production planning, product structures, material inventory, lot traceability, packing, delivery, invoicing, payment collection, accounting exports, and reporting in one operational system.

How does Streamline help specialty manufacturers?

Streamline helps connect customer demand, production dates, product structures, materials, labels, inventory lots, finished goods, delivery documents, invoices, payments, and reporting so teams do not rebuild the same data across spreadsheets.

Can Streamline connect orders to production planning?

Yes. Standing orders, cart orders, mobile orders, and imported orders can flow into production planning, material demand, inventory updates, delivery records, and invoices.

Does Streamline support formulas, product structures, labels, and costing?

Yes. Streamline supports recipe-driven and formula-style production, production cards and summaries, material usage, item and product costs, labels, and finished goods workflows.

Can Streamline help with lot tracking and traceability?

Yes. Streamline supports goods-in, supplier lots, internal lot numbers, stock counts, stock adjustments, production usage, finished goods, delivery records, and recall-ready traceability.

Can specialty manufacturers use Streamline for delivery and distribution?

Yes. Streamline supports pick and pack, route management, delivery notes, packing slips, driver reports, proof of delivery, delivery asset tracking, delivery charges, and delivery-to-invoice workflows.

Is Streamline only for one type of manufacturer?

No. Streamline is best suited to order-driven manufacturers and distributors that need customer orders, production, inventory, traceability, delivery, finance, and reporting connected in one workflow.
