What it does

Run wholesale distribution without chasing the order after it arrives.

Wholesale distribution moves fast: repeat customers, changing quantities, customer-specific prices, stock availability, delivery routes, packing documents, invoices, credits, and payments all have to line up before the next truck leaves.

Streamline gives distributors one connected workflow for customer service, warehouse, purchasing, dispatch, drivers, finance, and management.

What is wholesale distribution ERP software? It is the operating system for B2B distribution: order intake, customer pricing, inventory, replenishment, Pick & Pack, routes, delivery records, invoicing, accounts receivable, payment collection, accounting exports, and reporting in one place.

FAQ

Wholesale distribution ERP software questions, answered.

What is wholesale distribution ERP software?

Wholesale distribution ERP software connects B2B ordering, customer pricing, inventory, replenishment, Pick & Pack, route delivery, invoices, payments, accounting exports, and reporting in one system.

How does Streamline help wholesale distributors?

Streamline helps distributors manage recurring orders, customer-specific catalogues and pricing, stock movement, purchasing needs, route-ready picking, delivery documents, invoices, A/R, payments, and reports from one connected workflow.

Can Streamline support direct store delivery?

Yes. Streamline supports route management, Pick & Pack, delivery notes, packing slips, delivery charges, driver reports, route optimization integrations, proof of delivery, and delivery asset tracking.

Can customers have different prices, SKUs, and catalogues?

Yes. Streamline supports customer-specific pricing, price lists, item visibility, customer catalogues, customer SKUs, customer addresses, address-level access, and customer-specific document settings.

Can Streamline help with lot tracking and stock rotation?

Yes. Streamline supports goods-in, receiving, stock counts, adjustments, lot details, use-by dates, FEFO stock selection, barcode labels, finished goods, delivery records, and traceability workflows.

Can Streamline connect deliveries to invoices?

Yes. Completed deliveries and customer pricing can generate draft invoices for review, then flow into customer invoice libraries, payment collection, A/R, credit notes, accounting exports, and reports.

Does Streamline integrate with accounting tools?

Yes. Streamline supports QuickBooks Online workflows for pairing, exports, imports, invoices, credit notes, customers, items, vendors, and payments. It also supports accounting export workflows.

Is Streamline only for bakeries?

No. Bakery is a proven Streamline strength, but the platform is built for order-driven production and distribution businesses, including wholesale distributors, food and beverage distributors, route-based suppliers, and producer-distributors.
