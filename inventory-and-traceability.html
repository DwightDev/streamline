What it does

Manage bakery stock without the guesswork.

Streamline combines bakery inventory management software and bakery traceability software in one connected system. That means ingredient receipts, stock counts, lot records, barcode labels, production usage, and delivery traceability all stay linked.

Instead of using one tool for inventory and another for traceability, Streamline keeps both in the same workflow — which is a lot nicer when audits, recalls, and stock questions show up before coffee.

FAQ

Inventory management questions, answered.

Does Streamline include inventory management software for bakeries?

Yes. Streamline supports goods receipt, purchase invoice scanning, lot number assignment, barcode label printing, stock moves, inventory counts, stock adjustments, ingredient depletion, and purchase order replenishment — all connected to production planning and traceability.

How does Streamline handle lot tracking?

Lots are assigned at goods-in and linked to vendor lot numbers. Those lot records flow automatically through recipe production, finished goods, packing slips, and dispatch scanning — giving complete traceability from ingredient to customer delivery without separate software.

Can Streamline trigger purchase orders from stock levels?

Yes. Streamline generates shopping lists based on current stock, par levels, and recipe demand from live customer orders. You can create purchase orders directly from those shopping lists and send them to suppliers from within the system.

Does Streamline support food safety traceability requirements?

Yes. Streamline records lot numbers, use-by dates, delivery temperatures, vendor relationships, recipe lot usage, packing slip traceability, and dispatch scanning. Recall simulations and traceability reports can be generated to support audits and food safety management.

How does inventory management connect to production planning?

Current stock levels appear directly in the production planning view alongside order quantities, par levels, and forecasts. Production runs automatically deplete ingredient lots, keeping stock levels accurate without manual reconciliation.
