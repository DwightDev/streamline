What it does

Control stock without spreadsheet cleanup.

Wholesale inventory management software should answer three questions fast: what is in stock, where it is, and what needs action next. Streamline keeps those answers connected to sales, production, purchasing, delivery, and reporting.

What is wholesale inventory management software? It is software that helps wholesale and order-driven businesses track stock levels, locations, receiving, counts, adjustments, purchasing, and movement across warehouses, production areas, routes, and customer orders.

Receive

Record items, suppliers, quantities, lots, use-by dates, and storage locations as stock arrives.

Store

Keep stock organized by location, item type, unit, status, and available quantity.

Count

Run stock counts, review variances, and update inventory with clearer records.

Use

Adjust inventory when orders ship, production runs, recipes are made, or stock moves.

Replenish

Turn stock needs, par levels, usage, and demand into smarter purchase actions.

Report

Review stock movement, inventory value, usage, shrinkage, and purchasing needs.

FAQ

Wholesale inventory management software questions, answered.

What is wholesale inventory management software?

Wholesale inventory management software helps businesses track stock levels, locations, receiving, counts, transfers, adjustments, replenishment, purchase orders, and inventory reports across wholesale and distribution workflows.

Is Streamline only for bakeries?

No. Streamline has strong wholesale bakery and food production experience, but the inventory workflow also supports wholesalers, manufacturers, commercial kitchens, prepared foods teams, route distributors, and multi-site operators.

Does Streamline support barcode inventory?

Yes. Streamline supports barcode workflows for faster stock intake, labels, picking, and inventory identification.

Can Streamline manage lots and expiry dates?

Yes. Streamline can track supplier lots, internal lot numbers, use-by dates, stock locations, and stock movement. This helps teams keep inventory useful for traceability and freshness control.

Can Streamline help reduce stockouts?

Yes. Streamline connects inventory with purchasing, par levels, usage, demand, and purchase order workflows so teams can replenish before shortages disrupt orders or production.
