How it works

Export. Optimize. Bring the route back into distribution.

Streamline stays the operational source for delivery addresses, order data, packing slips, delivery notes, and distribution reports. Route optimization tools help calculate the route. The practical goal is simple: make it easier to pick, pack, load, dispatch, and deliver in the right order.

Create and close delivery-ready orders in Streamline.
Use distribution views to prepare Pick & Pack, delivery notes, and packing slips.
Send supported delivery orders to the route optimization tool.
Optimize the route in OptimoRoute or Detrack based on your delivery workflow.
Use the optimized order to support route-based packing, loading, dispatch, and reporting.

Built for operational distribution, not just map routing

Streamline’s delivery workflow is tied to customers, addresses, delivery rules, route order, packing slips, driver reports, invoices, and proof of delivery. That makes route optimization part of the wider delivery and distribution process.

OptimoRoute Integration

Send Streamline delivery orders to OptimoRoute for route planning.

The OptimoRoute integration supports route planning for Streamline distribution workflows. From a supported Distribution page, teams can use More > Send to OptimoRoute, then check OptimoRoute to confirm the orders were imported.

  • Send delivery orders from Streamline to OptimoRoute
  • Use route optimization software for route planning and scheduling
  • Support efficient delivery sequences for drivers and dispatch
  • Plan routes while keeping operational records in Streamline
  • Use optimized routes to support route-order packing and loading
  • Connect routing decisions to Streamline distribution workflows

Streamline Distribution

Route optimization works best when delivery data is already organized.

Streamline’s Distribution module gives teams the operational structure around the route: route manager, Pick & Pack views, delivery notes, packing slips, delivery charges, driver reports, proof of delivery, and delivery asset tracking.

Route Manager

The Route Manager lets teams organize routes in delivery order. That order controls the sequence of Distribution reports, helping teams pack orders in delivery order before dispatch.

  • Organize delivery routes
  • Support route-order Pick & Pack
  • Prepare route-based driver reports
  • Control packing slip and delivery note workflows

Operational benefit

Route optimization should reduce delivery friction, not create another disconnected workflow. Streamline keeps the route connected to customer addresses, packing slips, delivery notes, invoices, and driver reporting.

Delivery rules and charges

Streamline can apply delivery rules by invoice period, delivery, or address level. Delivery fees can adjust based on order value and rule settings, supporting more flexible distribution pricing.

  • Delivery rules by address
  • Delivery charges by order value
  • Route-aware delivery settings
  • Finance-connected delivery workflows

FAQ

Route optimization integration questions, answered.

Does Streamline integrate with OptimoRoute?

Yes. Streamline supports sending orders to OptimoRoute from supported Distribution pages using More > Send to OptimoRoute. After sending, teams should confirm the orders were imported in OptimoRoute.

Does Streamline support Detrack?

Yes. Streamline supports Detrack as part of its route optimization and delivery management integration set. Position Detrack around delivery tracking, route planning, customer notifications, and electronic proof of delivery workflows where configured.

What does Streamline keep as the source of truth?

Streamline remains the operational system for customers, addresses, orders, delivery notes, packing slips, delivery rules, route reporting, invoices, and distribution workflows. Route tools help optimize delivery sequence and driver execution.

Can Streamline help teams pack in delivery order?

Yes. The Route Manager controls the sequence of Distribution reports, so orders can be organized and packed in delivery order.
