Stock Count Overview

Run stock counts from the floor, not after the fact.

Stock Counting is one of the three core Inventory App workflows, alongside Goods Receipt and Recipe Production. This page focuses only on the counting process: selecting what to count, recording quantities, reviewing variances, and syncing the final adjustment.

Mobile Counting

Count stock by location, category, or warehouse.

Teams can start a stock count from the mobile app and work through the items that matter for that count. The workflow keeps counting close to the shelves, cold room, dry store, or warehouse area being checked.

  • Count by location, category, or full warehouse
  • Scan items or select them from the count list
  • Enter counted quantities directly on the device
  • Keep warehouse and production teams connected to Streamline

Move Stock records stock transfers between locations so inventory reflects where goods are actually stored.

Take Stock

Take Stock removes stock from available inventory when items are taken out outside the normal recipe production flow.

  • Select the item being taken from stock
  • Enter the quantity removed
  • Add a reason for the stock movement
  • Sync the stock reduction to Streamline ERP

FAQ

What does Stock Counting do in the Streamline Inventory App?

It lets users count stock from a mobile device, enter quantities, review variances, add adjustment reason codes, and sync approved inventory updates back to Streamline ERP.

Can users count by location?

Yes. The Inventory App supports counting by location, by category, or by full warehouse depending on the stock count being run.

Does the app support blind counts?

Yes. Blind count mode is included to support more unbiased counting before users review variances against system stock.

Is this the same as Goods Receipt?

No. Goods Receipt is for receiving incoming goods and assigning lots. Stock Counting is for checking existing stock and posting approved adjustments.
