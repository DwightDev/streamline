Automatic validation

Validate every request before it becomes an order.

Before an order is imported, the extension checks whether:

  • The order was submitted before the deadline
  • The delivery address is served on the requested delivery date
  • Requested products are available or made on the selected day
  • Required order details are complete
  • The order meets all your business' fulfillment rules

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Chrome Extension do?

It scans approved Gmail order emails, processes PDF, HTML, CSV and Excel order files, validates order details, imports valid orders into Streamline, and flags emails that need attention.

Does it work with Outlook or other email clients?

The Streamline Chrome Extension only works on Gmail in Google Chrome. If you use another email client, you may easily create a Gmail address, specifically to receive orders. Either autoforward order emails to the new address or ask customers to send orders directly to the new address.

Does it work in Edge, Safari, or other browsers?

The Streamline Chrome Extension only works on Google Chrome and it does not work on Edge, Safari or other browsers. Click here to download Google Chrome.

What do the order statuses mean?

Statuses show whether an order was successfully imported, needs review, failed, had already been entered in Streamline (duplicate not entered), was ignored, or has only been scanned.

How does this connect to Streamline?

Valid order details are imported into Streamline, while exceptions are flagged so your team can review them before moving forward.
