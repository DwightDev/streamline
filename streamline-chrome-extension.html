Common questions

What does the Streamline Inventory App do?

The Streamline Inventory App lets your warehouse and production team receive goods, assign internal lot numbers, link vendor lot references, print barcode labels, count stock by location, move inventory, and record recipe production runs — all from an Android mobile device.

Is the app included with Streamline ERP?

Yes. The Streamline Inventory App is included at no extra cost as part of your Streamline ERP subscription. It connects directly to your live operational data.

Does the app work offline?

The app supports core scanning and data entry functions in low-connectivity environments, with automatic sync when connection is restored.

Can the app print barcode labels directly?

Yes. The app connects to compatible label printers and can print lot-specific barcode labels at goods receipt, during stock movements, and at recipe production time.

How does the app connect to traceability?

Every lot assigned at goods receipt, every stock movement, and every recipe production run recorded in the app automatically updates the traceability record in Streamline ERP — creating an unbroken chain from supplier to customer delivery.
