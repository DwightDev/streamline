What it does

Follow every ingredient from supplier to customer.

FAQ

Traceability questions, answered.

Does Streamline include bakery traceability software?

Yes. Streamline connects goods-in records, lot numbers, vendor lot references, use-by dates, production usage, finished goods, packing slips, invoices, and delivery records in one bakery ERP system.

How does Streamline handle ingredient lot tracking?

Ingredient lots are assigned at goods-in and stay linked through production. Finished goods receive their own lot numbers while remaining connected to the original ingredient lots used.

Can Streamline help identify the oldest lot?

Yes. Julian date-based lot numbers make older lots easier to recognize, and FEFO suggestions help production select the lot with the nearest expiry date first.

Can lot numbers appear on packing slips and invoices?

Yes. Lot numbers can appear on packing slips and invoices to help trace what was delivered and to whom.
