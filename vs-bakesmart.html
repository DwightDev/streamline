The key difference

Wholesale ERP depth vs modular retail bakery operations.

BakeSmart has useful bakery tools for retail ordering, ecommerce, custom cake pricing, wholesale menus, recurring orders, and invoice payments. Streamline stands out when a growing wholesale bakery needs one connected ERP system where customer ordering, production, recipes, inventory, traceability, delivery, finance, CRM, reporting, and service teams all work from the same operational data.

FAQ

Common questions about Streamline vs BakeSmart.

What is the best BakeSmart alternative for wholesale bakeries?
Streamline is a strong BakeSmart alternative for wholesale bakeries that need connected ordering, standing orders, production planning, recipe costing, inventory, lot traceability, route planning, proof of delivery, delivery asset tracking, invoicing, CRM, accounting workflows, and reporting in one bakery ERP system.
Is Streamline bakery ERP software?
Yes. Streamline is bakery ERP software for wholesale bakeries, food manufacturers, distributors, commissaries, and central kitchens. It connects order management, production, recipes, inventory, purchasing, traceability, labelling, allergens, nutrition facts, delivery, invoicing, accounts receivable, CRM, reporting, and customer service workflows.
Does Streamline handle traceability?
Yes. Streamline tracks lots from supplier goods-in through recipes, finished goods, packing slips, customer deliveries, and recall workflows. This helps bakeries and food manufacturers connect ingredient traceability with production, delivery, and customer records.
Which platform is better for retail bakeries and custom cakes?
BakeSmart may be a good fit for retail bakeries that prioritize POS, ecommerce, custom cake pricing, decorator tickets, gift cards, texting, and customer-facing online orders. Streamline is stronger when retail or ecommerce orders need to feed a larger wholesale ERP workflow across production, inventory, traceability, delivery, finance, CRM, and reporting.
