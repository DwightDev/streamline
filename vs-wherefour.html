The key difference

Bakery order-to-invoice ERP vs manufacturing traceability ERP.

Wherefour offers a strong manufacturing ERP foundation for inventory, MRP, production, lot tracking, compliance, warehouse, ecommerce, and accounting workflows. Streamline stands out when a bakery needs customer ordering, production, recipe management, lot traceability, delivery, proof of delivery, reusable delivery assets, invoicing, CRM, customer service, and reporting connected in one bakery-specific operational system.

🛒

Wholesale bakery ordering depth

Streamline supports customer-specific catalogues, pricing, SKUs, delivery addresses, address permissions, standing orders, saved carts, cloned orders, order deadlines, lead times, late orders, and mobile ordering.

🏭

Production tied to bakery recipes

Orders can drive production lists, recipe totals, recipe cards, ingredient demand, batch planning, inventory depletion, allergens, nutrition facts, labels, and lot records.

🚚

Delivery and finance stay connected

Route planning, packing slips, proof of delivery, reusable delivery assets, unprocessed invoices, credit notes, statements, payment collection, and accounting workflows all connect back to the same order data.

FAQ

Common questions about Streamline vs Wherefour.

What is the best Wherefour alternative for wholesale bakeries?
Streamline is a strong Wherefour alternative for wholesale bakeries that need connected online ordering, standing orders, production planning, recipe management, inventory, lot traceability, route planning, proof of delivery, reusable delivery asset tracking, invoicing, CRM, accounting workflows, and reporting in one bakery ERP system.
Is Streamline bakery ERP software?
Yes. Streamline is bakery ERP software for wholesale bakeries, food manufacturers, distributors, commissaries, and central kitchens. It connects order management, production, recipes, inventory, purchasing, traceability, labelling, allergens, nutrition facts, delivery, invoicing, accounts receivable, CRM, reporting, and customer service workflows.
Does Streamline handle traceability?
Yes. Streamline tracks lots from supplier goods-in through recipes, finished goods, packing slips, customer deliveries, and recall workflows. This helps bakeries and food manufacturers connect ingredient traceability with production, delivery, customer, and invoice records.
Which platform is better for delivery-heavy wholesale bakery operations?
Streamline is designed for delivery-heavy wholesale bakery operations that need route planning, packing slips, driver notes, proof of delivery, signatures, photos, geotags, timestamps, and reusable delivery asset tracking connected to orders, invoices, and customer accounts.
